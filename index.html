Pricing (estimate)

Total = item prices + mileage + extra trips + difficulty add-ons

Mileage
  • First 10 miles: FREE
  • After 10 miles: $0.50 per additional mile
Weight add-on
  • Every 100 lbs over 200 (single item) is + $20
Item count discounts
  • 1 item: full price
  • 2nd item: $5 off
  • 3rd+ items: $10 off
  • Bulk sale (8+ items, small items only): $18 each item
Base item pricing
  • Small: $30 (+$2.50 each additional floor)
  • Medium: $50 (+$7.50 each additional floor)
  • Large: $75 (+$10 each additional floor)
  • Appliance: $100 (+$15 each additional floor)

FAQ

Do you assemble or disassemble items?
We will NOT assemble/disassemble complex or antique utilities/furniture (stoves, some bed frames, complex washer/dryer sets). If you’re unsure, send a photo and we’ll tell you quickly.
How does mileage pricing work?
First 10 miles are free. After 10 miles, it’s $0.50 per additional mile.
What should I send for a fast estimate?
Pickup and dropoff area, item list, floors/stairs (and elevator), and any tight spaces. Photos help a lot.

FAQ

Do you assemble or disassemble items?
We will NOT assemble/disassemble complex or antique utilities/furniture (stoves, some bed frames, complex washer/dryer sets). If you’re unsure, send a photo and we’ll tell you quickly.
How does mileage pricing work?
First 10 miles are free. After 10 miles, it’s $0.50 per additional mile.
What should I send for a fast estimate?
Pickup and dropoff area, item list, floors/stairs (and elevator), and any tight spaces. Photos help a lot.
